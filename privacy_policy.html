Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cleaning As Aussies. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, phone number, mobile phone, postal code, and a description of the cleaning service you require. This information is used to follow up on inquiries, provide the requested services, and communicate with you about our offerings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and follow up on your inquiries.
  • To provide the cleaning services you've requested.
  • To communicate with you about similar services and promotional offers.
  • To gather feedback after the service is completed.
  • To improve our website and services.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on both cloud providers and our local servers. Access to this data is restricted to specific individuals within our organization who are responsible for service follow-ups and direct service provision.

Data Retention

We retain customer data based on the services they've availed. If there's potential for future service or offers, we securely store the data. Otherwise, customer data is deleted after the service is completed.

User Rights

Users can request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data. Since our website doesn't have user authentication or cookies, this process is straightforward.

Third-Party Services

We don't share customer information with third-party organizations and don't use third-party services that access user data.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any changes to our Privacy Policy will be updated on our website. Customers with whom we have contact details will be notified directly.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@caozs.com.
